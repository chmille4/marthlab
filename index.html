iobio

realtime genomic data visualization and analysis web tools

  • gene.iobio - annotate and prioritize variants
  • genepanel.iobio - generate disease- and phenotype-associated genes
  • bam.iobio - alignment quality control
  • vcf.iobio - variant calling quality control
  • oncogene.iobio - annotate and track variants in oncology studies
